A Visit England 4 Star accredited self-catering holiday apartment ideally situated in a quiet area right in the heart of Broadstairs town centre, yet only two minutes' walk from its many local bars, cafes and restaurants and the picturesque sandy beach at Viking Bay. Property
Amenities

The apartment is decorated to a high standard and offers an open plan kitchen/living space, master bedroom with en suite shower room, second bedroom with full size single bunk beds, a family bathroom and private courtyard.

Important
Information

There are a few steps leading down to the apartment which may not be suitable for a guest with limited mobility. For further details please read our Accessibility Guide.

Smoking is not permitted in the property or communal areas and unfortunately we are unable to accept pets.

This property is most suited to families and couples.

Arrival & departure days are usually Saturday to Saturday during peak season - other days may be suitable so please ask.

Minimum of 7 night stays during peak seasons.
